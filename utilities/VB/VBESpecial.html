If you aren't sure about something, take a minute and read throught the questions below, you may find your answer there. If not, try asking in the #help_and_questions channel at the Digitama Hatchery Discord Server.
  • How do I get Vital Values?
    • Your steps and your heart rate both contribute to your Vital Values. In addition, winning battles will increase them, while losing battles will decrease them.
  • How long will it take my Digimon to evolve?
    • Current Stage Time to Next Stage
    I 1 Hour
    II 3 Hours
    III 12 Hours
    IV 24 Hours
    V 24 Hours
    VI 3 Hours
    VII N/A
    Unless you kill it first, your Digimon is guaranteed to evolve into Stage III. If a Stage III, IV or V Digimon has fulfilled its specific requirements for reaching Stage IV, V or VI respecitvely, it is guaranteed to reach that stage. The time your Digimon spends sleeping counts towards its evolution timer, but time spent in backup will not. Turning the Vital Bracelet off will also pause all functions, including the evolution time. If evolving via Jogress, you do not need to have the appropriate backup Digimon for the entire evolution timeframe, it only needs to be in backup at the time of evolution. When evolving via Jogress, only the active Digimon evolves. The Digimon in backup remains in backup exactly as it was, and does not influence the stats of the evolved Digimon. Jogress evolution takes the same amount of time as a normal evolution for that stage.
  • What carries over after evolution?
    • Increases to BP, HP and AP will remain after evolution. Vital Values will reset after evolution for every stage except when evolving from from Stage I to Stage II or from Stage II to Stage III. PP, Battles and Win Ratio reset after every evolution.
  • A requirement doesn't mention a specific stat, how do I know how much of that stat I need?
    • The evolution requirements will only show stats are are actually required, hence them being called that. Not all stats are required for evolving, and if a specific stat isn't listed, this means it does not matter at all. For example, Gammamon does not list what Win Ratio is needed to evolve into Betel Gammamon. This is because it does not matter, Gammamon will evolve into Betel Gammon simply by having 10 or more Trophies, 1,000 or more Vital Values and 8 or more Battles, no matter what its current win ratio is.
  • What does it mean if the evolution requirements on this guide show multiple bullet points?
    • When you click on a Digimon in the guide, sometimes you will see bullet points listing multiple requirements. In these cases, you only need to choose one of the bullet points, as these are all independent requirements. For example, Betamon has three bullet points for evolving into Numemon. One reads "0-2 Trophies", one reads "0-499 Vital Values" and the last reads "0-2 Battles". You will meet the requirement by fulfilling either of those conditions upon reaching the evolution window, you don't necessarily need to fulfill all three.
  • If I meet the conditions for multiple evolution options, which one will I get?
    • For the most part, you will receive the evolution with the highest requirement for Vital Values, assuming you met the other requirements. If two evolutions have the same requirement for Vital Values, then you will receive the one with the highest requirement for Win Ratio. Technically speaking this is not a hard rule, as the true determining factor of which evolution will occur is the order in which the evolutions are listed in the code of the Dim card, but so far there have not been any Dim cards that have broken the pattern as described.
  • If I already cleared a stage and unlocked an evolution with a different Digimon, do I have to clear it again with the one I am currently raising?
    • Generally speaking, no. Your device stores the Stage Clear status after the first time you clear that area for that Dim Card. This means that any Digimon you raise after clearing that area for that Dim card will be able to evolve to evolutions that require that area to be cleared. However, this will not preserve after a factory reset. If you choose to start a brand new Digimon by inserting a Dim card, that new Digimon will have access to the locked evolution, but won't have access to the cleared areas themsleves.
  • What is a Jogress?
    • There are cases where a specific Digimon can Jogress with another specific Digimon on a specific Dim card. Once the appropriate Digimon has been placed in backup on the device, the time it takes for the Jogress to occur depends on the normal evolution time for that Stage, as shown in the above table. Please note the Digimon in backup must be from a specific Dim, even if the same Digimon appears on other Dim cards. Original Dim cards had additional Jogress options based on stage and attribute, but these will not occur on the Vital Bracelet BE, they can only activate on older Vital Bracelet models. When evolving via Jogress, only the active Digimon evolves. The Digimon in backup remains in backup exactly as it was, and does not influence the stats of the evolved Digimon.
  • What will cause my Digimon to die?
    • Getting injured 4 times in one day
    • Not wearing your Vital Bracelet for 24 hours (time spent sleeping or in backup does not count towards the 24 hours)

PP

How much PP is required to evolve. PP is gained by doing Squat Trainings.

Vital Values

How many Vital Values are required to evolve. Vital Values are earned based on your steps and heart rate. They increase when you win battles, but will decrease if you lose.

Battles

How many Battles are required to evolve. Battles can be triggered by touching the Vital Bracelet to any powered NFC source, such as a smart phone or vending machine.

Win Ratio

The minimum required percentage of battle wins to evolve.

N/A

Not Applicable. If any Digimon shows this as a requirement on the chart, it means there is no minimum requirement for that particular condition. If a Digimon shows N/A for all four conditions, this means that you will get that Digimon if you do no not meet the minimum requirements for any other evolution option.

Jogress with

Indicates which Digimon must be in backup in order to evolve. Only specific Digimon can Jogress, and this will occur based on the normal evolution times shown in the FAQ. Please note that attribute based Jogress evolutions used on the original Dim cards will not work on the Vital Bracelet BE.
